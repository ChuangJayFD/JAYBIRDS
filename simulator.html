✦ The Bottom Line — Why Both Votes Matter
  • The Standard Tally weights Japan at 70% — a structural disadvantage for international trainees
  • The Rescue Round exists, but your position depends on outranking other international trainees in SEKAI votes
  • Near-zero Kokumin votes means losing 30% of your score even in the SEKAI round
  • SEKAI votes alone are not enough. Kokumin votes must be built up continuously
